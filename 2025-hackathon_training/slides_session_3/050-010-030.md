# Technical Crisis Decision Tree

The mark of a good organizer is not preventing failure, but managing it gracefully.
- WiFi Failure: Deploy "Sneakernet" (USB drives) and extend deadlines; frame it as a "Retro Hacker Challenge."
- Platform Crash: Switch to Google Forms or paper submissions to avoid ruining the sense of achievement.
- Team Meltdown: Use the SOAR method (Stop, Observe, Assess, React) and separate students if necessary.
