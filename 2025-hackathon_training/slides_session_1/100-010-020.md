# The Hackathon Concept Canvas

**Task:** Create a **Hackathon Concept Canvas**. They must define:
- Hackathon Goals: Define the objectives, context and purpose of your hackathon.
- Hackathon Type: Define the type, theme, size and duration of your hackathon.
- Key Results: Define the type of results expected and the success criteria to evaluate them.
- Attendees: Define the target audience and the skills needed to join your hackathon.
- Attendeesâ€™ Value Proposition: Describe the preparation, the experience and the afterwards support of attendees.
- Key Partners: Define the sponsors, partners, stakeholders and suppliers.
- Key Resources: Define the key resources and roles needed for your hackathon.
