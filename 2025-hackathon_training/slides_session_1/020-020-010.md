# Pedagogy and Success Models

- Improvement of "Soft Skills" (Collaboration, Adaptability) alongside technical hard skills.
- PBL connection: The hackathon is a hyper-accelerated Project-Based Learning unit.
- Inquiry-Based: open-ended problem instead of defined ones with already known solutions.
- Game Jams: Focus on systems thinking and logic through game design.
- Social Impact Hacks: "Hack Your School" empowers students to fix local problems.
