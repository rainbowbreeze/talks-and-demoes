# The "Why"

- Student Agency: Shifting from "passive consumers" to "active creators."
- Constructionism: Learning happens best when building a public artifacts.
- Iterative Learning: avoid students follow instructions without understanding the underlying logic
- Interdisciplinary Bridge: The convergence of Art (UI/UX), Language (Pitching), and Math (Logic) - STEAM.
- Constraint Creativity: Time limits force prioritization and negotiation skills.
