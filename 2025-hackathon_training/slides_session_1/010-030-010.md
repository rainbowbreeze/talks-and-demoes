# Origins and Evolution of the Hackathon

Definition: "Hack" (Exploratory programming) + "Marathon" (Endurance).

- 1999 Origins: Coined simultaneously by OpenBSD (Calgary) and Sun Microsystems.
- Cultural Shift: Moving from "subversive activity" to "collaborative innovation."
- The Three Eras: Pioneer (Open Source), Corporate (Facebook/Google innovation), Democratization (Student & Civic engagement).
